Test Plan for Dashboard Chart Fixes

1. Verify Trend Chart X-Axis
   - Go to "Incident Trend Last 30 Days" (or any trend chart).
   - Check X-axis labels. They should be in "MM-DD" format (e.g. "01-15") instead of "2026-01-15T00..."

2. Verify Bar Chart Labels
   - Go to "Incident Category Statistics" (or any bar chart).
   - Check the bars.
   - Numbers should be ABOVE the bars, not inside.
   - Numbers should be dark grey, not white.
   - Small bars should have visible numbers above them.
   - There should be NO duplicate numbers.
